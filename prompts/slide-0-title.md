# Title Slide: Monk OS — An Operating System Where Bun Is the Hardware

## Style Brief

Blueprint / Cyanotype aesthetic. Deep indigo/navy blue background (#1a2744) with faint grid lines and subtle fold creases like a well-used architectural drawing. All line art in white and light cyan (#7ec8e3) with chalk-like hand-drafted strokes and visible construction marks. Warm amber/gold (#d4a843) used sparingly for callouts, pinned annotations, and emphasis. Bold hand-lettered uppercase headings in white/cream with slightly uneven baselines. Thin cyan leader lines with small labels. Subtle blueprint paper grain and worn edges. No photographs, no 3D renders, no color beyond navy/cyan/amber. 16:9 aspect ratio. No people, no faces.

Amber-gold accents on pinned notes and emphasis only. 16:9 aspect ratio. The feel of a master blueprint cover sheet for a complete system design.

Below that, a thin amber rule, then smaller text: "Plan 9 philosophy. BeOS queryable files. message-driven kernel."
